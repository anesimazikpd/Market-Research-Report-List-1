<p><p>The banjo dulcimer market features two primary types: all solid wood and laminated wood instruments. All solid wood dulcimers are crafted entirely from solid pieces, offering superior sound quality and resonance, often favored by purists and professionals for their rich tone. On the other hand, laminated wood dulcimers utilize layers of different woods glued together, providing enhanced durability and affordability. These laminated models are ideal for beginners or those seeking a budget-friendly option without sacrificing playability.</p></p>
