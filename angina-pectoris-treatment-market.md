<p><p>The Angina Pectoris Treatment Market consists of various drug classes aimed at relieving angina symptoms and improving heart function. Antiplatelet agents prevent blood clots, while beta-adrenergic blocking agents reduce heart workload. Calcium channel blockers relax blood vessels, enhancing blood flow. Short-acting and long-acting nitroglycerines alleviate chest pain by dilating arteries. Angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitors lower blood pressure and reduce strain on the heart. Anti-ischemic agents further improve oxygen delivery to heart tissues, collectively addressing angina's underlying causes.</p></p>
