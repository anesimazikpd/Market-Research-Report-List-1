<p><p>A mandibular distractor is a medical device used in orthognathic surgery to reposition the lower jaw (mandible) in patients with jaw deformities, facial discrepancies, or developmental issues. The device gradually elongates bone through mechanical distraction, enabling new bone formation in the gap created. This technique is favored for its minimally invasive nature and ability to improve facial aesthetics and functional outcomes for patients with conditions such as micrognathia or congenital anomalies.</p><p>The Mandibular Distractor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
