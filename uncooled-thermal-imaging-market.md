<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR</li><li>Mid Wave IR</li><li>Long Wave IR</li><li>Far Wave IR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal imaging market is segmented into four types based on infrared wavelengths. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) typically covers 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for visibility in low light and detecting moisture. Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, excels in military applications and gas detection. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR), between 8 to 14 micrometers, is commonly used in surveillance and industrial inspections. Far Wave Infrared (FWIR) extends beyond LWIR, focusing on niche applications in research and development.</p></p>
