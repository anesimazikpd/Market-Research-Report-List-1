<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Crude Oil Tankers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Panamax(60000~80000DWT)</li><li>Aframax(80000~120000DWT)</li><li>Suezmax(120000~200000DWT)</li><li>VLCC(200000~300000DWT)</li><li>ULCC(Above 300000DWT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crude oil tankers are categorized by their deadweight tonnage (DWT), which influences their market dynamics. Panamax tankers (60,000-80,000 DWT) can transit the Panama Canal. Aframax tankers (80,000-120,000 DWT) are efficient for medium-haul routes, typically servicing regional markets. Suezmax vessels (120,000-200,000 DWT) are optimized for the Suez Canal. VLCC (200,000-300,000 DWT) and ULCC tankers (above 300,000 DWT) are suited for long-haul transportation, carrying large volumes from oil-producing regions to refineries worldwide. Each type serves distinct demand patterns based on capacity and voyage efficiency.</p></p>
