<p><p>Energy-based ablation devices are classified into several types based on their energy sources. Electrical ablation uses electrical currents to destroy tissue, while light-based devices utilize lasers to target specific cells. Radiation ablation employs focused radiation to eliminate unwanted tissue. Radiofrequency devices generate heat through electromagnetic waves, effectively abating tissue, whereas ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves for precision targeting. Other market types may include cryoablation, which uses extreme cold, and microwave ablation, utilizing microwave energy for tissue destruction.</p></p>
