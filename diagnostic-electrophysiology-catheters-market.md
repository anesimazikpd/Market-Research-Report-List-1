<p><p>The diagnostic electrophysiology catheters market includes circular mapping catheters and grid mapping catheters, which are essential tools for cardiac arrhythmia diagnosis and treatment. Circular mapping catheters provide a comprehensive view of electrical activity in specific areas of the heart, aiding in the identification of abnormal signals. Grid mapping catheters offer a high-resolution, multi-electrode interface that captures detailed spatial information about cardiac conduction. Together, these catheters enhance the precision of electrophysiological studies and improve patient outcomes in cardiac interventions.</p></p>
