<p><p>The pet medicine market is divided into two key categories: external use and internal use medications. External use products include topical treatments such as ointments, creams, and sprays that are applied directly to the skin or fur for issues like infections, parasites, or skin irritations. Internal use medications encompass oral or injectable medicines aimed at treating internal conditions, infections, or chronic illnesses. Both segments are essential for ensuring the overall health and well-being of pets, addressing their diverse medical needs.</p></p>
