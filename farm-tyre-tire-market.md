<p><p>The farm tire market primarily consists of two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature plies arranged at various angles, offering a sturdy build but less flexibility, making them suitable for older equipment. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged perpendicularly, providing improved traction, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. Radial tires are increasingly preferred for modern machinery due to their enhanced performance and longevity. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, influencing farmers' tire choices.</p></p>
