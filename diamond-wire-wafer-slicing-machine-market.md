<p><p>The Diamond Wire Wafer Slicing Machine market is primarily divided into two types: Single Wire Slicing and Multi Wire Slicing. In contrast, Multi Wire Slicing employs multiple wires simultaneously, enhancing production efficiency and throughput while reducing material waste.
