<p><strong>The Automatic Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angle Seat Piston Valve</li><li>Globe Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Pinch Valve</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic control valves market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Angle seat piston valves offer high flow rates and are suitable for steam and liquids. Globe valves provide precise flow control, often used in throttling applications. Butterfly valves excel in regulating large volumes, typically in HVAC and water systems. Ball valves feature quick shut-off capabilities, ideal for on/off control. Pinch valves are excellent for handling slurries, while diaphragm valves are favored for sanitary applications, ensuring tight shut-off and contamination control.</p></p>
