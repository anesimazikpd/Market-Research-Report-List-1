<p><p>The milk powder market is primarily categorized into Skim Milk Powder (SMP) and Whole Milk Powder (WMP). Skim Milk Powder is produced by removing the fat content from milk, resulting in a lower-calorie option rich in proteins, making it popular in health-conscious food applications. Whole Milk Powder retains all the natural fat from milk, offering a creamier texture and richer flavor, commonly used in confectionery and baking products. Both types serve diverse functions across various culinary sectors and food processing industries.</p></p>
