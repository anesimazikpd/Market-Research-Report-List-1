<p><p>The PVC leather market comprises various types, including Calender PVC Leather and Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather. Calender PVC Leather is produced by forcing PVC through rollers to create a smooth, flexible film used in upholstery and fashion. Conversely, Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather involves applying a liquid PVC mixture onto a substrate via a rotary screen, allowing for intricate patterns and designs, ideal for various applications like accessories and automotive interiors. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
