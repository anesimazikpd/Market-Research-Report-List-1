<p><p>The chip beads market is categorized into high impedance and low impedance types, each serving distinct electronic applications. High impedance chip beads are designed to suppress high-frequency noise while maintaining minimal DC resistance, making them ideal for sensitive RF circuits. Conversely, low impedance chip beads exhibit higher currents with reduced resistance, suitable for power applications where noise filtering is still necessary. Both types enhance signal integrity, ensuring reliable performance in various electronic devices and circuits.</p></p>
