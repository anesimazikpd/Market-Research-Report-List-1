<p><strong>The CNG Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CNG Solenoid Tank Valves</li><li>CNG Manual Tank Valves</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNG valves market is primarily categorized into three types: CNG solenoid tank valves, CNG manual tank valves, and others. CNG solenoid tank valves are electrically operated devices that control gas flow automatically, enhancing safety and efficiency in refueling operations. CNG manual tank valves require manual operation, offering a simple and cost-effective solution. The "others" category includes specialized valves like check valves and pressure relief valves, which serve specific applications in CNG systems, ensuring performance and safety across various setups.</p></p>
