<p><ul><li>Loose Ball Bearings</li><li>Retainer Bearings</li><li>Sealed Cartridge Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle bearings market features three main types: loose ball bearings, retainer bearings, and sealed cartridge bearings. Loose ball bearings consist of individual balls that offer low cost and easy maintenance but require more frequent adjustments. Retainer bearings have balls held in a cage, providing improved stability and ease of installation. Sealed cartridge bearings feature a complete unit sealed against dirt and moisture, offering low maintenance and enhanced durability. Each type caters to different preferences and riding conditions, impacting performance and longevity.</p></p>
