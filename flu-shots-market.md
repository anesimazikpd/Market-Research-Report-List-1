<p><p>The flu shots market can be categorized into three main types: Influenza Virus Split Vaccines, which contain virus particles that are split to stimulate an immune response without causing disease; Influenza Virus Subunit Vaccines, which consist of purified proteins from the virus that trigger immunity; and Influenza Whole Virus Vaccines, which use inactivated whole viruses to provoke a robust immune response.
