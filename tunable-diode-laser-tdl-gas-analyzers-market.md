<p><p>Tunable Diode Laser (TDL) gas analyzers are utilized for precise gas detection and monitoring in various applications. The **in-situ** market involves direct measurement of gas concentrations within the process environment, offering real-time data without sample extraction, which enhances response times and reduces the risk of contamination. In contrast, the **extractive** market requires sampling gases from the process for analysis in a separate location. This approach allows for more detailed analysis but may introduce delays and potential sample degradation. Both methods cater to different operational needs and environments.</p></p>
