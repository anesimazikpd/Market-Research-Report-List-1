<p><ul><li>Rotary Distributor Pump</li><li>Individual Control Pump</li><li>Unit Injection</li><li>Common Rail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection pumps market encompasses various technologies that control fuel delivery in engines. Rotary distributor pumps use a rotating mechanism to distribute fuel to multiple cylinders, ensuring even distribution. Individual control pumps manage fuel flow to each cylinder separately for precise control. Unit injection systems integrate injectors and pumps for optimized performance, while common rail systems provide a shared fuel rail, allowing for multiple injections per cycle, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. These systems cater to different engine types and performance needs.</p></p>
