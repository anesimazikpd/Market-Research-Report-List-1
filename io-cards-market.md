<p><p>IO cards, or Input/Output cards, are essential components in industrial automation, facilitating data acquisition and control. The market is segmented into Digital IO cards and Analog IO cards. Digital IO cards manage discrete signals, enabling on/off device control and simple logic operations, making them ideal for applications like switches and relays. In contrast, Analog IO cards handle continuous signals, capturing data from sensors and enabling precise process control, suited for applications in temperature, pressure, and flow monitoring.</p></p>
