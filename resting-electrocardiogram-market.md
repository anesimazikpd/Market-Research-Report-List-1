<p><p>The resting electrocardiogram (ECG) market is categorized into three main types: single lead, 3-6 lead, and 12-lead systems. Single lead ECGs are portable, user-friendly devices, ideal for basic heart monitoring. The 3-6 lead ECGs provide a broader view of cardiac activity, useful for more detailed assessments. In contrast, 12-lead ECGs offer comprehensive data by recording signals from multiple angles, enabling accurate diagnosis of various heart conditions. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing market demand and growth.</p></p>
