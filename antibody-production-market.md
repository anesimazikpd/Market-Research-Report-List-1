<p><p>The antibody production market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Polyclonal antibodies** are derived from multiple B cell lines and recognize various epitopes, making them useful for diverse applications.</p><p>2. **Monoclonal antibodies** originate from a single B cell clone, targeting specific antigens with high specificity and consistency.</p><p>3. **Murine antibodies** are produced in mice and serve as a foundation for many therapeutic applications. </p><p>4. **Chimeric antibodies** combine mouse and human elements to enhance efficacy while reducing immunogenicity.</p><p>5. **Humanized antibodies** are mostly human, retaining only necessary mouse components for improved compatibility in therapeutic uses.</p></p>
