<p><p>The Sisal Twine Market is categorized into two main types: Natural Form (Unoiled) and Treated (Oiled). In contrast, Treated (Oiled) sisal twine is coated with oils or chemicals to enhance durability, water resistance, and longevity, often preferred for heavy-duty uses. Both types serve various industries, including agriculture, crafts, and packaging, catering to different performance and sustainability needs.</p></p>
