<p><strong>The Efuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Latched Type eFuse</li><li>Auto-retry Type eFuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eFuse market is categorized into two main types: latched type eFuse and auto-retry type eFuse. Latched type eFuses maintain their OFF state until manually reset, providing a reliable protective measure against overcurrent and fault conditions. In contrast, auto-retry type eFuses automatically reset after the fault is cleared, allowing for continuous operation without manual intervention. Both types are essential in various applications, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial systems, ensuring device safety and reliability.</p></p>
