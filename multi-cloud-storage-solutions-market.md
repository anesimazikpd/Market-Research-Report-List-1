<p><p>Multi-cloud storage solutions encompass three main market types: public, private, and hybrid. Public cloud storage offers scalable resources over the internet, managed by third-party providers, ideal for businesses seeking cost-effective solutions without physical infrastructure. Private cloud storage is dedicated to a single organization, providing enhanced security and control, suitable for sensitive data. Hybrid cloud combines both public and private services, enabling organizations to balance flexibility and control by leveraging the benefits of both environments for optimal data management.</p></p>
