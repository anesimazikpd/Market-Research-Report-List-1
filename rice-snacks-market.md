<p><p>The rice snacks market can be broadly categorized into two types: glutinous rice-based and non-glutinous rice-based snacks. Glutinous rice snacks are known for their chewy texture and are often used in traditional Asian treats such as rice cakes and mochi. In contrast, non-glutinous rice snacks, made from regular rice varieties, tend to have a crispier texture and include products like rice crisps and popped rice snacks. Both categories cater to diverse consumer preferences, offering various flavors and health benefits.</p></p>
