<p><p>The jewelry laser marking machine market includes three primary types: CO2 laser marking machines, Fiber laser marking machines, and UV laser marking machines. CO2 laser machines are ideal for organic materials and non-metal surfaces, providing high precision. Fiber laser marking machines excel in metal engraving, delivering speed and durability. UV laser marking machines are suitable for delicate materials, offering high-resolution markings with minimal heat impact, making them perfect for intricate designs. Each type serves unique applications in the jewelry industry.</p></p>
