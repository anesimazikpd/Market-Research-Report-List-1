<p><p>Composite floor panels are categorized based on their core materials, each offering unique benefits. Aluminum core panels provide high strength and durability, ideal for heavy-load applications. Honeycomb core panels are lightweight and offer good stiffness, making them suitable for various constructions. Foam core panels are known for excellent thermal insulation and are lightweight, often used in commercial applications. Balsa core panels provide a balance of weight and strength, commonly used in aerospace. Other types include various innovative materials enhancing performance.</p></p>
