<p><p>The Patient-Controlled Analgesic (PCA) Infusion Pumps market comprises two main types: syringe-type and cassette-based fluid delivery systems. </p><p>Syringe-type pumps utilize pre-filled syringes to deliver a controlled dose of analgesics, allowing for easy monitoring and adjustment. In contrast, cassette-based pumps use disposable cassettes that store and dispense medication, often featuring advanced technology for enhanced programming and safety. Both types facilitate self-administration of pain relief by patients, improving pain management outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
