<p><p>Affiliate marketing software platforms operate primarily through three market types: Cost Per Sale (CPS), where affiliates earn a commission for driving sales; Cost Per Lead (CPL), where affiliates are compensated for generating leads or sign-ups; and Cost Per Click (CPC), where affiliates receive payment for each click on a linked advertisement.
