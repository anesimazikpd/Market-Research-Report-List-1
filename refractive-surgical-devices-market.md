<p><p>The refractive surgical devices market primarily encompasses femtosecond lasers and excimer lasers, along with other technologies. Femtosecond lasers utilize ultra-short light pulses to create precise corneal incisions for procedures like LASIK, enhancing accuracy and reducing recovery time. Excimer lasers use ultraviolet light to reshape the cornea's surface, effectively correcting refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Other devices may include implants and manual surgical tools, contributing to the overall advancement of vision correction techniques in this rapidly evolving market.</p></p>
