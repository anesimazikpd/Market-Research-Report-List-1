<p><p>The UV curing equipment market is primarily divided into two types: microwave-powered systems and arc lamp UV systems. Microwave-powered systems utilize microwave energy to generate ultraviolet light, offering higher efficiency and energy savings while reducing heat output. In contrast, arc lamp UV systems employ electrical arcs to produce intense UV light, widely used for traditional curing applications. Both technologies cater to various industries, including printing, coatings, and adhesives, enhancing production speed and quality through fast curing processes.</p></p>
