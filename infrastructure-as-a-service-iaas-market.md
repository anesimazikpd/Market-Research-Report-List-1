<p><p>Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is a cloud computing model that provides virtualized computing resources over the internet. Users can scale resources up or down based on demand, enabling flexibility and efficiency in managing IT environments.</p><p>The IaaS market is experiencing notable growth, driven by increased adoption of cloud services, digital transformation initiatives, and the need for cost-effective IT solutions. Organizations are increasingly leveraging IaaS to enhance operational efficiency, reduce maintenance burdens, and accelerate time-to-market for their applications. The growing trend of remote work and the rise of emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), and big data analytics are also fueling demand for IaaS solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, as cybersecurity concerns rise, businesses are opting for scalable and secure IaaS offerings that provide robust protection against data breaches.
