<p><p>Gelling Fiber Dressings are advanced wound care products designed to manage exudate and promote an optimal healing environment. Additionally, the aging population and rising incidences of conditions like diabetes and obesity are contributing to this market expansion. Healthcare providers increasingly prefer these dressings for their effectiveness in promoting faster healing and minimizing complications.</p><p>Emerging trends include the development of bioactive materials that enhance the therapeutic properties of dressings, as well as an emphasis on eco-friendly and biodegradable options.
