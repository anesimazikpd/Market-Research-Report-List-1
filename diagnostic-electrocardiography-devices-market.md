<p><p>Diagnostic electrocardiography devices encompass various types that cater to different cardiac monitoring needs. Holter monitor systems provide continuous heart rhythm recording over 24-48 hours. Stress ECG devices assess heart performance under physical stress. Resting ECGs capture cardiac activity in a stationary state. Single-lead and 3-6 lead systems focus on basic monitoring, while 12-lead ECGs offer comprehensive heart function analysis. Additional devices in the market serve specialized applications, enhancing the ability to diagnose and track heart conditions effectively.</p></p>
